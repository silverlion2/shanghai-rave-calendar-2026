Vivat Football: Football & Electronic Theatre Experience

Vivat Football (Vivant Football) is a contemporary stage production at Theatre YOUNG on August 7 and 8, 2026. It combines physical theatre, freestyle football performance, and a live electronic music score created in real time by DJ/musician Badfocus. Performed in Czech with Chinese/Czech subtitles, recommended for ages 6+. Structured like a live match where movement, rhythm, and sound interact continuously.
