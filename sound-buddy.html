What it hears

Volume, low-end weight, top-end texture, broad pulse, energy movement, and approximate style lane.

What it avoids

No artist recognition, no lyrics, no fingerprinting, no saved audio, no public room recording, no exact track ID.

Best use

Run it with an official DJ set, a platform stream, or music you have permission to analyze.

How to read it

Treat every output as a listening prompt: compare the cue to what you hear, then use the style board for vocabulary.
